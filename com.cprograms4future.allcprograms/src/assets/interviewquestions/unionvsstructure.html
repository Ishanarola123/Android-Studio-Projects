Question:

Difference between union and structures

Answer:
Structures Unions
keyword used for Structures is "struct" keyword used for Unions is "union"
Occupy more memory.Memory size=Sum of size of it's memebers Occupy less memory.Memory size=Size of largest member
Can access all members at a time Can access only one member at a time
Altering a member will not have any impact on other members Altering a member will affect other members
struct Book
{
int bookId;
char bookName[100];
};
 union Book
{
int bookId;
char bookName[100];
};
